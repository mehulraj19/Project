About YogaAsan Star


In order to promote correct Yoga postures, a ‘yogasana star’ digital game based using image processing approach could be conceptualized which will be able to determine/examine than correctness of any Asan (e.g.: straightness of back, angles and positions of hands and legs, etc.) and give scores accordingly. Using camera, the game should scan and identify the Asan in real-time based on posture and then examines it for correctness.



Our Vision


Our world is growing in a tremendous speed. Everyday we can see new things happening all around us, from new buildings to new gadgets, and we all are so involved with this everchanging environment, to know what's happening around us and keep us to up to date. We socialize, we learn, we grow everyday and keep ourselves fit for this enviorment. And the pandemic come into our lives and we have to bound ourselves within four walls of our home to keep ourselves and our loved ones safe.

Many of us have lost their lives, their loved ones, their jobs and are in state where it is very difficult to keep mental health in right place. Here, we come into picture. All of us like playing games, like to spend time with our families and friends and play simple games.

Our Vision is to promote YOGA and give a platform to people where they can playfully compete with our families and friends by doing different Yoga. With enjoyment, we are trying to help people have a better Mental Health and what's better than yoga for doing this job.
